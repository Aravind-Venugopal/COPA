Users: Geo Information & Devices

Users who visited the COPA website from April 2nd to 18th.

Location & Language

Google Analytics recognized users' IP addresses so we can know where the website visitors come from. For the COPA website, most visitors are from the United States. Canada, United Kindom, and Australia also have many users. The map on the right shows the average bounce rate of each country. We can see that the country with a few users would have a higher bounce rate.

COPA website's visitors from 97 countries/regions. The United States is the major location of users, taking about 82.52% of the total users. Other countries include Canada (2.84%), German (2.28%), Australia (2.11%), GB (2.02%), and so on. The top city is Boardman.

People from Europe tend to have a higher bounce rate, fewer page views per session, and less session duration than Americans, especially Southern and Eastern Europe. Eastern Asia also has a similar pattern to Europe. The potential reason could be the language they use is not English.

English (including en-us, en, en-gb, en-ca, en-au) is the most popular language used in the website, taking 93.23% proportion of users. Other languages are Germany (2.08%), French (0.59%), Portuguese (0.36%), Spanish (0.3%), Dutch, Italian, Chinese, and so on.

Devices & Operation Systems

Desktop users are about 62.72% of the total users. Mobile is about 27.98%, and tablet is about 9.31%. Mobile users have a higher bounce rate (25.43%) much higher than others. Their page views per session (7.99) also fewer than others. Desktop users tend to have a longer session duration than mobile and tablet users.

Mobile & Tablet

Per mobile brandings, most people used Apple iPhones (86.91%) to visit the website. Huawei, Motorola, Xiaomi, and LG users have a higher bounce rate and fewer page views. We plotted all mobiles into a scatter chart where the y-axis measures the bounce rate, and the x-axis is transformed the number of users. If there are some mobile devices appear on the top-right of the plot, COPA administrators need to make the website more compatible to those devices. Currently, although there are many devices have a high bounce rate, there are not many users use those devices, so the problem is not big or even existing.
